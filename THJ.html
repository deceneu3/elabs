Mission

The Honey Journey

Thehoneyjourney is a decentralized NFT game platform which provide blockchain services to players, beehive adopters, beekeepers, and communities, consumers all over the world, through the Play and Earn system. Thehoneyjourney is developed on BSC network and plans to drive the evolution of online gaming morphed into targeted industry and focused on solutions to help promising producers, projects and products reach their full potential both in real world and in the future metaverse worlds.

Ask Anything

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions we constantly get, mostly from new adopters.

Do I receive real honey for stacking THJ Tokens?

Yes. We unite the digital with the real world by providing real pure honey made by the bees, for every hive our adopters are stacking THJ Tokens.

Example: for stacking 50,000 THJ Tokens your reward is 1Kg of real honey every month.

Is there an annual maintenance fee?
Yes but optional. All the adopters can continue after the 2nd year of adoption with a small fee of only € 55 per year, ensuring to keep the hives protected and well taken care of, keeping their adopter privilege and continue to receive top quality honey.
Is the honey tested?
Yes, all our honey is tested in more than one laboratory on a constant basis, for a better quality check. We can also provide the medical and export certificates if requested when delivering the product to our THJ adopters. Our product is top quality, rare, pure and organic raw honey, directly from the hive.
How much it costs to withdraw honey?
We allow you to use any shipping company you prefer (DHL, FEDEX, ANY), there is no problem with us! Their price usually depends on the volume and weight and the distance. Every adopter pays the shipping and packaging costs. Receiving the honey to your physical address depends on the packaging type and the shipping company of your choosing. We fulfill every order and give the goods to any courier/shipping company you send to pick it up.
